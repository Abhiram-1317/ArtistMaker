## Creating Your First Movie

### Step 1: Start a New Project

From your Dashboard, click **"Create New Movie"** or navigate to **Projects → New Project**.

### Step 2: Define Your Concept

- **Title** — Give your movie a compelling name
- **Genre** — Choose from Sci-Fi, Fantasy, Horror, Animation, Drama, and more
- **Description** — Write a brief synopsis (2-3 sentences works great)
- **Mood/Tone** — Select the emotional tone: dark, uplifting, suspenseful, etc.

### Step 3: Design Characters

You have two options:
- **AI Generate** — Select your genre and click "AI Generate" to get pre-designed characters that fit your story
- **Add Manually** — Create characters from scratch with custom names, descriptions, and traits

### Step 4: Choose Your Style

Select the visual style for your movie:
- Resolution (1080p, 4K)
- Aspect ratio (16:9, 2.39:1 Cinematic, etc.)
- Color palette
- Art direction

### Step 5: Review & Create

Review all your settings, then click **"Create Project"** to generate your movie!

## FAQ

### How long does movie generation take?
Generation time depends on the length and complexity of your movie. Short clips (1-2 minutes) typically take a few minutes. Full-length movies may take longer.

### Can I edit my movie after generation?
Yes! The Editor allows you to refine every aspect of your movie — script, characters, scenes, and audio — even after the initial generation.

### What formats can I export in?
Movies can be exported in MP4 (H.264) at up to 4K resolution.

### Is my content private?
By default, all projects are private. You choose when and if to make them public.

### How do credits work?
Each generation uses credits based on the output length and quality. Free accounts receive a starting balance. PRO and Enterprise plans include more credits and faster generation.

## Troubleshooting

### "Connection lost" error
Check your internet connection and try refreshing the page. Your work is auto-saved.

### Movie generation stuck
If generation appears stuck for more than 10 minutes, try:
1. Refresh the page
2. Check the project status in your Dashboard
3. If the issue persists, contact support

### Can't log in
1. Make sure you're using the correct email
2. Try resetting your password
3. Clear your browser cache and cookies
4. Try a different browser

### Page not loading
- Clear your browser cache
- Disable browser extensions
- Try incognito/private mode
- Check our [status page](https://status.projectgenesis.ai) for outages
